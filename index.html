About the USA

The United States is a country located in North America. It gained its independence from Great Britain on July 4th, 1776. Ever since then, the United States has grown and it has become such a powerful nation and an influential leader in finances, agriculture, and military. It is currently led by President Joe Biden, and it currently has 50 states, a federal district, and many territories.

The first president of the United States was George Washington. He was a general for the Army, and he is very famous for his time as a general, especially when he led his men across the Delaware River on Christmas Day, 1776. It is still one of the most famous pictures in United States history.
